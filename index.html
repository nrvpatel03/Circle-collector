Circle Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.
Each circle below has a random hidden value attached to it.
Each time you click a circle,that random value will be added to your total score.
The goal is to match your score with the random number given.
If your score exceeds the number, you lose. If they match, you win.
